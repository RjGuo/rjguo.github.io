Research Interests

My research interests focus on theoretical study of the electron transport in materials. By investigating the electron-phonon, electron-electron, and electron-defect interactions via first-principle theory, I compute and analyze the transport properties such as mobility and saturation velocity. These studies can help improve electronic and optoelectronic devices.
